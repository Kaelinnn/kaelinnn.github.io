Welcome to my portfolio

Geographic Information Systems (GIS) consist of software and databases used to analyze and display spatial data. In contrast, Geographic Information Science (GIScience) focuses on developing and refining the techniques for analyzing and managing spatial data. We can use GIS to help solve complex problems in fields like urban planning, environmental management and even public health. The information in this portfolio focuses on Social Development Indicator 15 - Life on Land.
